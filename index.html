Company Overview

Florel was founded by Austin Dobrusky and Madalyn Viger. They had a passion for gardening in which they practiced together in their hometown of Seward, Nebraska. When they met, they immediately bonded over their love for working with flowers. In 2006, they decided to open a flower shop in downtown Seward, and they have been in the gardening business ever since. Over twenty different types of flowers and seeds are sold in every Florel location across the state. Check out our gallery of flowers here!
